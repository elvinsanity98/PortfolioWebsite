About Me

A Path of Growth and Innovation

Hello, I'm Elvin Jhon Gimena, a 25-year-old IT professional with a deep passion for technology and creativity. I graduated from Ave Maria College with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. My career began as a Graphic Artist at Duque Print Hauz, where I had the opportunity to merge my design skills with technical expertise. Later, I transitioned into the role of Assistant Trainer at Ave Maria College for TESDA's Computer System Services program, where I spent a year helping others enhance their IT skills.,

Currently, I’m proud to serve as the IT Head at Rural Bank of Liloy (ZN), Inc. In this role, I lead our IT initiatives and work to ensure that our systems are running smoothly and securely. My journey in the IT field has been both challenging and rewarding, and I’m excited about the future and the opportunities that lie ahead.
